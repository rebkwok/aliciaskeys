About Alicia

Alicia has always been fascinated by and passionate about the mind/body link. This led her to studies in both fields, holding qualifications in fitness and psychology. Her approach unique as takes what she has learned and applies it to her practice. Alicia started her career as a fitness instructor; seeing the impact that exercise had on mental health she did her first degree in Health Psychology. Alicia had always been interested in Yoga but never found something that worked for her so when a centre she taught in her asked her to train in and then teach it, she took the plunge and hasn’t looked back since. That was 15 years ago!!! Since then she has gained 2 further post graduate degrees in psychology. In her psychological practice, Alicia has worked across disciplines and has also worked to support professionals in their day to day work. In April 2015, Alicia developed her own approach to flexibility and mobility and began working with athletes on improving performance and preventing injury. Not one to sit on her laurels, she is constantly studying and developing new techniques. She learns from the best in the world to enhance her own training and therefore her teaching. Alicia’s goal has always been to develop a practice which links body and mind, knowing that she has the ability to support people to work through a wide range of challenges. So now, Alicia’s keys is born to provide a sanctuary, support and strategies for the best possible outcomes.
